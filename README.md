# 📊 Sales Insights Dashboard – AtliQ Hardware

## 📌 Project Summary
This project is an **end-to-end Power BI analytics solution** built for **AtliQ Hardware**, a computer hardware company operating in a dynamically changing market.

The objective of this project is to **transform raw sales data into actionable insights** through an interactive Power BI dashboard, enabling the sales team and management to monitor performance, identify revenue trends, and support **data-driven decision making**.

The dashboard provides a **centralized view of revenue, profit margins, sales quantity, market performance, customer contributions, and product performance** across multiple years.

# 📊 Insights Delivered

- Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing **₹985M revenue, ₹24.7M profit, and 2M sales quantity** across four years to monitor key business KPIs.

- Performed **market-level profitability analysis**, identifying **Delhi NCR as the largest revenue contributor (52.8%)**, **Bhubaneshwar with the highest margin (10.48%)**, and **Bengaluru with negative profitability (-20.8%)**.

- Evaluated **customer and product performance**, identifying **Electricalsara Stores (₹413M revenue)** and **Product 318 (₹69M revenue)** as major revenue drivers.

- Identified a **significant revenue decline in June 2020**, highlighting potential external or seasonal business impacts.
